Choosing an ENT Specialist:

  • Identify Your Problem: Hearing loss, ear infections, dizziness, sinus issues, nasal blockage, allergies, voice change, throat pain, snoring, swallowing difficulty, neck lump, or suspected head/neck cancer?
  • Sub-specialty Match: Seek focused expertise for cochlear implants, complex sinus/skull base surgery, voice problems, balance disorders, or cancer.
  • Pediatric Needs: A Pediatric ENT specialist is often preferred for children.
  • Diagnostic Facilities: Ensure access to Audiometry, Endoscopy (nasal, laryngeal), CT/MRI, possibly VNG or Stroboscopy.
  • Surgical Expertise: Inquire about the surgeon's experience with specific procedures (FESS, Tympanoplasty, Tonsillectomy, Thyroidectomy, etc.).
