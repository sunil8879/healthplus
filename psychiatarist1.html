Choosing a Psychiatrist:

  • Your Needs: Depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, addiction, OCD, PTSD, child behavior issues, geriatric mental health?
  • Therapy vs. Medication: Psychiatrists (MDs) can prescribe medication; many also offer therapy, while others work with psychologists/counselors for therapy.
  • Sub-specialty: Consider specialists in Child & Adolescent, Geriatric, Addiction, Forensic, or Consultation-Liaison Psychiatry for specific issues.
  • Comfort Level: Crucial to find a psychiatrist you feel comfortable talking to openly.
  • Referral: Your GP or a psychologist can often provide referrals.
