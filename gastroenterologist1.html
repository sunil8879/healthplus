A representative list of recognized specialists. Not an objective ranking. Verify DM/DNB qualifications independently.

Choosing a Gastroenterologist/Hepatologist:

  • Symptoms & Condition: Identify issues like acidity, pain, jaundice, GI bleeding, diarrhea/constipation, liver disease, IBD, etc.
  • Sub-specialty Need: Seek focused expertise for advanced endoscopy (ERCP, EUS), liver transplant evaluation, complex IBD, or pancreatic diseases.
  • Diagnostic & Therapeutic Tools: Ensure access to endoscopes, ERCP/EUS capabilities, FibroScan, etc., if needed.
  • Referral: Your GP can often provide a referral.
  • Hospital Affiliation: Consider hospital reputation and facilities for procedures or admissions.
