Choosing an Orthodontist:

  • Credentials: Verify the orthodontist holds an MDS degree in Orthodontics & Dentofacial Orthopedics. BDS is not sufficient for specialization.
  • Experience: Look for experience relevant to your needs (adults, children, specific appliances like aligners, lingual braces).
  • Consultation: Discuss treatment options, duration, costs, risks, and view before/after photos of similar cases.
  • Technology: If interested in aligners or digital scanning, confirm clinic experience.
  • Clinic Environment: Choose a comfortable clinic with professional staff for long-term treatment.
