A representative list of recognized doctors. Not an objective ranking. Verify MD/DNB qualifications independently.

Choosing a General Physician / Internal Medicine Specialist:

  • Primary Care vs. Specialist: Internists often serve as primary care doctors for adults, especially those with multiple health issues, and are skilled diagnosticians.
  • Nature of Problem: Consider if you need chronic disease management, diagnosis of symptoms, routine check-ups, or acute illness care.
  • Sub-specialty Interest: An Internist with focus in areas like diabetes, infectious diseases, or geriatrics might be beneficial for specific conditions.
  • Communication & Approach: Choose a doctor you are comfortable with and who involves you in decisions.
