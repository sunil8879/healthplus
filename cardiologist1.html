Choosing a Cardiologist:

  • Sub-specialty: Identify if you need an Interventional Cardiologist (stents), Electrophysiologist (rhythm), Non-invasive Cardiologist (diagnosis), Cardiac Surgeon (surgery), or Pediatric Cardiologist.
  • Condition: Match the doctor's expertise to your specific condition (e.g., coronary artery disease, heart failure, arrhythmia, valve disease).
  • Experience & Volume: For complex procedures, consider the surgeon's/cardiologist's experience and hospital volume.
  • Hospital Facilities: Ensure the hospital has appropriate facilities (Cath Lab, ICU, OT, Cardiac Rehab).
  • Second Opinion: Often recommended for major diagnoses or procedures.
