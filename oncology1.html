Top 100 Oncologists (Cancer Specialists) in India

A representative list including Medical, Surgical & Radiation Oncologists. Not an objective ranking. Verify MD/DM/MS/MCh/DNB qualifications independently.

Choosing an Oncologist:

  • Type of Oncologist: Medical (chemo, immuno), Surgical (removes tumors), Radiation (radiotherapy), or specialized (Gynae, Uro, Pediatric, etc.)? Your primary doctor or initial consult will guide this.
  • Cancer Type Expertise: Ensure the oncologist has experience with your specific type and stage of cancer.
  • Hospital Infrastructure: Comprehensive cancer centers offer integrated care (surgery, radiation, medical oncology, pathology, radiology, support services).
  • Multidisciplinary Team (Tumor Board): Complex cases benefit from discussion among specialists.
  • Research & Clinical Trials: Major centers may offer access to clinical trials.
  • Second Opinion: Highly recommended, especially for complex diagnoses or treatment plans.
