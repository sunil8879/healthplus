A representative list including Clinical Immunologists, Rheumatologists, Allergists & PID experts. Not an objective ranking. Verify DM/DNB/PhD qualifications independently.

Choosing an Immunologist or Related Specialist:

  • Correct Specialist: Rheumatologists for autoimmune diseases; PID specialists for recurrent infections; Allergists for allergies; Transplant team for transplant immunology. Clinical Immunology is often integrated within these.
  • Condition Focus: Match specialist's expertise (SLE, RA, Vasculitis, PID, Asthma, etc.) to your condition.
  • Diagnostic Needs: Ensure access to specialized blood tests, allergy testing, genetic testing if required.
  • Treatment Approach: Discuss options (immunosuppression, biologics, IVIG, allergy management).
  • Referral: GPs or other specialists often refer to the appropriate expert.
