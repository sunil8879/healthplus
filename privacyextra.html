Health Plus Privacy Statement

This privacy statement outlines how Health Plus collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our health application.

We collect information you provide directly to Health Plus, such as your name, email, and health-related data (e.g., weight, activity levels, symptoms). We protect this data with industry-standard encryption.

Use of Information

  • Analyze and improve App functionality.

Contact Us
