A representative list of recognized dentists and specialists. Not an objective ranking. Verify BDS/MDS qualifications independently.

Choosing a Dentist:

  • Identify Your Need: General check-up, cleaning, root canal (Endodontist), braces (Orthodontist), implants/dentures (Prosthodontist), gum treatment (Periodontist), extraction (Oral Surgeon), child care (Pedodontist)?
  • Credentials: Look for BDS (basic degree) and MDS (Master's degree for specialists in a specific branch).
  • Clinic vs. Hospital: Choose based on preference and complexity of the required treatment.
  • Consultation: Discuss concerns, treatment plan, costs, and feel comfortable with the dentist's approach.
