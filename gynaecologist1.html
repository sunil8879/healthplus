Choosing a Gynaecologist:

  • Your Needs: Routine check-ups, pregnancy care (low/high-risk), infertility treatment, specific surgery (laparoscopic, robotic), or treatment for conditions like PCOS, fibroids, cancer?
  • Sub-specialty: Consider specialists in High-Risk Obstetrics, Infertility (IVF), Gynae Oncology, Urogynaecology, or Minimally Invasive Surgery if needed.
  • Comfort Level: Choose a doctor you feel comfortable discussing personal health matters with.
  • Hospital Affiliation: Consider the hospital's reputation, facilities (NICU, OT), and insurance coverage.
