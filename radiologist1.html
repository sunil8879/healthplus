A representative list including Diagnostic & Interventional Radiology, Neuroradiology, Nuclear Medicine etc. Not an objective ranking. Verify MD/DNB/DM qualifications independently.

Choosing Radiology Services:

  • Sub-specialty Expertise: Crucial for complex scans (Neuro MRI, Cardiac CT, PET-CT, MSK MRI, Breast Imaging). Ensure the interpreting radiologist has relevant expertise.
  • Interventional Radiology: A separate procedural field. Choose based on expertise in specific interventions (biopsy, embolization, angioplasty).
  • Technology: Scanner quality (MRI Tesla strength, CT slice count, Digital X-ray) impacts image detail.
  • Accreditation: Look for accredited centers (NABH/NABL).
  • Referring Doctor's Input: Your doctor often recommends trusted centers/radiologists.
  • Patient Interaction: Primarily for Interventional Radiology procedures; diagnostic reports go to the referring doctor.
