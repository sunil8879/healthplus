A representative list of highly regarded specialists. Not an objective ranking. Verify details & qualifications independently.

Choosing a Bariatric Surgeon:

  • Credentials & Experience: Look for surgeons with specific fellowship training (Bariatric/MAS) and high volume experience. Membership in OSSI or IFSO is a good sign.
  • Hospital Accreditation: Choose surgeons operating in accredited facilities with good infrastructure (ICU, etc.).
  • Multidisciplinary Team: Ensure the program offers comprehensive care involving dietitians, psychologists, physicians, etc.
  • Procedures Offered: Discuss which procedure is suitable (Sleeve, Bypass, etc.) and confirm surgeon's proficiency.
  • Long-Term Follow-up: Understand the clinic's protocol for lifelong follow-up.
  • Consultation: Discuss goals, risks, benefits, alternatives, and ensure comfort with the surgeon and team.
