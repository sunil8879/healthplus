Choosing a Plastic Surgeon:

  • Board Certification: Ensure surgeon holds M.Ch. (Plastic Surgery) or DNB (Plastic Surgery). Membership in IAAPS/APSI is a good sign.
  • Specialty Match: Choose expertise relevant to your desired procedure (Facial Plastic, Breast Surgery, Body Contouring, Rhinoplasty, Reconstructive).
  • Experience & Photos: Ask about experience with the specific procedure and review before/after photos of their actual patients.
  • Hospital Accreditation: Ensure surgery is performed in an accredited facility with proper safety protocols.
  • Consultation: Discuss goals, realistic expectations, risks, recovery, and costs thoroughly. Ensure comfort and trust.
