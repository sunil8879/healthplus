Choosing an Ophthalmologist:

  • Your Condition: Cataract, Glaucoma, Retina issue (Diabetic Retinopathy, Macular Degeneration), Cornea problem, LASIK/Refractive Surgery need, Squint (Strabismus), Pediatric eye issue, Oculoplasty (eyelid/orbit)?
  • Sub-specialist: Seek specific expertise (Retina Specialist, Glaucoma Specialist, Cornea Specialist, etc.) for complex conditions.
  • Diagnostic & Surgical Technology: Inquire about available technology (OCT, Fundus Camera, Lasers, Phaco machines, Femtosecond Laser) if needed.
  • Hospital/Centre Reputation: Consider the eye hospital or clinic's facilities and reputation.
  • Referral: Optometrists or general ophthalmologists often provide referrals.
