Choosing a Neurologist:

  • Symptoms are Key: Headache, seizures, tremors/stiffness, weakness, numbness, dizziness, memory loss, vision changes, speech problems?
  • Sub-specialty Relevance: Seek specific expertise for conditions like Parkinson's, MS, Epilepsy, Stroke, Myasthenia Gravis.
  • Diagnostic Facilities: Ensure access to Neuroimaging (MRI/CT), EEG, EMG/NCS, and other specialized tests if needed.
  • Multidisciplinary Team: Complex conditions benefit from centers with strong Neurosurgery, Neuroradiology, Neuropsychology, and Rehab departments.
  • Second Opinion: Often recommended for chronic or complex neurological diagnoses.
